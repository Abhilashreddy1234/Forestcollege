Hostel - Boys & Girls

FCRI is fully residential campus providing accommodation for both boys and girls students. The hostels are meant for both boarding and lodging. The students resident of the hostels shall have all the facilities like 24 hour supply of water and electricity, gym equipment, tv room, CCTV Surveillance facility, multiple Newspapers availability and safe drinking water.

Both the hostels are supervised by warden and 2 caretakers to look after the requirements of the students. The rooms of the hostels are given a suitable study environment and space ensuring development and interaction among the students with the management of turning them independent and self reliable.
