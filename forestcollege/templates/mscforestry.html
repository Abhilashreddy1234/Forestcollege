PG program specializations:

As per the BSMA committee recommendations of ICAR, the PG degree nomenclature will be as follows:

  1. M.Sc. Forestry (Silviculture & Agroforestry)
  2. M.Sc. Forestry (Forest Biology & Tree Improvement)
  3. M.Sc. Forestry (Forest Resource Management)
  4. M.Sc. Forestry (Forest Products & Utilization)
  5. M.Sc. Forestry (Wildlife Sciences)

Department of NRM

This specialization offers a multidisciplinary approach, wherein students learn techniques that can be used to ensure the long-term sustainability of the forests and plantations.

The specialization equips the students with an array of resource management skills including Sustainable Forest Management, Forest health management, Urban green space management, Watershed management, Forest Soils and fertility management, Wasteland management, and Ecosystem management. The course is supported by the advance management tools such as remote sensing & GIS and Ecosystem services accounting.
