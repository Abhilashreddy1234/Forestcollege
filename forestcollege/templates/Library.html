Library

Libraries are the hub of academic life of the campus. The libraries are housed in spacious halls and well stacked with text books, technical journals, periodicals and reference material for advanced research and development work. Modern aids to education, including online digital libraries, Audio/Video records of renowned academicians are available in campus in different blocks of libraries. It remains open 14 hours a day from 8am to 10pm on all days, and up to 18 hours a day during examinations. The digital library and student study centre for general studies are available.
