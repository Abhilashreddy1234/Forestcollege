FCRI Study Circle

FCRI Study circle was established with an objective of guiding students of FCRI for future careers. Students are mentored for career development under the aegis of FCRI Study Circle, by providing guidance for various P.G. entrance exams like ICAR AIEEA etc. and various competitive exams like IAS/IFS etc. With these efforts, our students had topped various entrances and got PG admissions in prestigious national universities. Two of our students were also admitted into Auburn University (USA) along with full-tuition fee waiver and stipend of $ 1400 per month. 6 of our students cleared various UPSC exams like IAS, IFS, CDS, CAPF (AC) in their very first attempt and got selected for interview.

Apart from the above, FCRI study circle arranges various orientation/skill development events like- Guest Lectures, Topper Talks, Orientation Sessions etc.; Soft Skills Development (Communication skills, debates, Elocution, Group Discussions, interviewing skills, etc.) and providing coaching for Civil Services & IFS exam and other Govt. Jobs and also preparing them for MSc/ICAR entrance exams.
