Museum

Forest College and Research Institute (FCRI) is the first institute offering forestry education in entire Telangana state to bring out qualified professionals in forestry. It is necessary to establish a world-class museum covering all themes of forestry-related aspects. It will be beneficial for the general public, students, and farmers of Telangana State and the entire nation.

It is based on the forestry themes which exhibit the history and existence of forestry in India, forest types of India and Telangana State, State animal, State bird, State tree of Telangana, soil types, rocks, minerals, ecosystem services, different types of seeds, wood fossils, wood species of Telangana state, bamboo products, insect collections, etc.

Emerging as a World Class Institution.

Forestry Education, Research, Management, and Outreach.
