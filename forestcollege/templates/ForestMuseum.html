Museum

Forest College and Research Institute (FCRI) is the first institute offering forestry education in entire Telangana state to bring out qualified professional in forestry. It is necessary to establish a world class museum covering all themes of forestry related aspects. It will be benefited for general public, students and farmers of Telangana State and entire nation.

It is based on the forestry themes which exhibits history and existence of forestry in India, forest type of India and Telangana State, State animal, State bird, State tree of Telangana, soil types, rocks, minerals, ecosystems services, different types seed, wood fossils, wood species of Telangana state, bamboo products, insect collection etc.
