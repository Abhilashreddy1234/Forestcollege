Central Instrumentation Facility 

FCRI has established the Central Instrumentation Facility (CIF) to cater the needs of research in basic, applied and advanced sciences. The CIF, FCRI houses various state of the art sophisticated equipments which are operated and maintained by qualified team of Scientists. This facility is open to all students, teachers, researchers and technical personnel from universities, academic and research institutions and industries. Charges will be levied for the usage of analytical facilities.
