Sri. A. Venkateshwarlu, ACF

Deputy Director, FCRI

Sri. A. Venkateshwarlu is Assistant Conservator of Forests. He is working as the Deputy Director of Forest College and Research Institute, Hyderabad at Mulugu. He has a total of 29 years of service with the Telangana State Forest Department. He is a graduate from Govt. Degree College, Siddipet and completed his M.B.A from UCC&BM, Osmania University, 2 year diploma in Forestry from GFRC Rajpipla, Gujarat. He worked in IT wing of Forest Department and contributed his best for the development of Forest Management Information System for which he was awarded with Hon’ble Chief Minister Medal. He acquired extensive knowledge in Medicinal Plants by serving in Telangana State Medicinal Plants Board for a period of 7.5 Years as Deputy Executive Officer. He has experience in rural development, urban forestry, landscaping works and extension activities.
