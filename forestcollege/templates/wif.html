Wood Identification Tool Kit

FCRI has released professional wood Identification tool kit box along with a user manual. The kit consists of wood samples of 30 commercially important tree species of Telangana. The manual describes various key identification features of wood for effective use. This will be an useful guide to students, teachers, scientists and all technical and non-technical professionals in the field.

Wood Sample Identification Tool Kit has been developed by Department of Forest Products and Utilization, Forest College and Research Institute Hyderabad.

This tool kit box will be available for sale by placing orders with FCRI and if any queries related to this, FCRI and its team of scientists can be approached for further information.
