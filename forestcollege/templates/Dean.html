Dr. S. J. Asha, IFS

Dean, FCRI Hyderabad

Pursued B.Sc with Botany, Zoology, and Chemistry.

Pursued M.Sc (Anthropology) from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam.

Pursued Ph.D (Tribal Development and Poverty alleviation Programmes) from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam.

Joined Forest Department as Assistant Conservator of Forests in 1999. Later promoted to Deputy Conservator, Conservator, and Chief Conservator of Forests.

Worked as District Forest Officer in Khammam, Guntur, Visakhapatnam, Karimnagar, and Sircilla.

Worked as Chief Conservator of Forests, Warangal Circle/Zone.

Initiated "One Gram Panchayat-One Nursery" as Special Commissioner, Haritha Haram.

Currently serving as Director, TS Forest Academy.

Emerging as a World Class Institution.
