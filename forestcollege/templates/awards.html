Awards @ FCRI

The following teaching and non-teaching staff were awarded certificates and cash prizes for their meritorious service by the Telangana State Forest Department on 15th Aug 2020 – Dr. Kapil Sihag, Assistant Professor, Department of Forest Products & Utilization; Ms. P. Nikitha, Assistant Professor, Department of Basic and Social Sciences; Mr. Raghu, FSO; Mr. Murali. Similarly, two other faculty members, Mrs. NS Srinidhi, Assistant Professor (Environmental sciences), Department of Forest Ecology and Climate Sciences, and Dr. B. Sreedhar, Associate Professor, Department of Basic and Social Science, have received meritorious certificates on the occasion of Republic Day, i.e., on January 26, 2021.
